AUTOTASK INPUT VALIDATION

Ever experienced issues with validating input for Autotask? A wrong work type on a time entry can lead to an embarassing complaint from a customer who recieved an invoice 3 times what they were expecting.

You may have an extensive library of LiveReports scheduled to catch such errors, but there is just one problem.

It's all in the past, like autotask screenshots, capturing errors that have already happened.

These minor errors can cause huge problems. Wouldn't it be great if you could stop these errors from happening? Stop it dead, at the source, by validating the input directly from the engineer. Your Autotask ticketing system deserves to be data-error free.

Autotask Validation utilises highly advanced and customisable back-end Autotask input validation. No matter how your business organises it's data or manages it's processes for Autotask, our application will be able to accomodate your validation testing requirements.

Remove any doubt of human error and be 100% confident of the data being entered into Autotask. Stop wasting time fixing errors that should have never happened.

Entering the wrong work type on a ticket or allocating the wrong contract can result in incorrect invoices being sent to your customers. Protect your reputation and validate the input. Improve your Autotask Service Desk efficiency by eliminating human error and stopping easy mistakes from being made.

NO CHANGE TO YOUR WORKFLOW

You won't have to break from your existing workflow that your engineers and sales staff have already gotten used to.

By utilizing LiveLinks you can configure Autotask to send the relevent data to the validation software for when you create tickets or other Autotask entities.

For example, create a new service desk ticket by going to LiveLinks -> Create Ticket rather than New -> Ticket.

Stay within Autotask but gain the added benefit of highly advanced and customizable input validation.

Conditional rules allow you to define when your validations trigger

Your staff shouldn't feel like they are contained within small boxes. Want to send just a warning instead of refusing to complete the action? No problem, you can also set individual validations to be mandatory or not.

REAL-TIME VALIDATION FEEDBACK

This application is built with the end user in mind. Get instant feedback on the Autotask object you are creating in real time as you are completing the form.

If your form input triggers one of the conditional arguments configured for a validation group, a helpful message will instantly appear with the relevent information to make sure you know what's gone wrong and what you need to do to fix it.

By hiding all of the validation configuration from the end user, there is far less setup required per employee and no additional training required. If you want to make sure specific scenarios are handled just the way your processes define, Autotask Validation gives you the freedom to do just that.

You can also define individual validation tests to be passed as either a warning, or mandatory.

A mandatory validation will prevent any action from being taken, the user will not be able to submit the form until the validation test has passed.

However, there may be situations where you might want to inform the user that something they have selected might not be quite appropriate, but it isn't mission critical and is acceptable to be fixed afterwards.
